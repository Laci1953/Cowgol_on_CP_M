A knight's tour is a sequence of moves of a knight on a chessboard
 such that the knight visits every square exactly once.
The computer will keep trying to generate such tours,
 until succesfuly completing one!

To compile:
cowgol -o -lcowgol misc.coo string.coo knight.cow

It works on any VT100 compatible terminal.

Run sample:

D>knight
